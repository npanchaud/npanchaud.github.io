About me

What started as a geographer's journey, then took a turn towards cartography and urban planning. I finally figured out I liked drawing and coloring plans and maps. My feet are now taking me into the wonderful and ever-changing world of web cartography and mapping. I also like to wander on the open data and open source trails.
Currently I am conducting research in cartoraphic functionality for non-expert users on geoportals. It involves creating smart functions that help creating more beautiful maps on the geoportal as well as opening cartographic knowledge.
Furthermore, I also teach graduate courses in web mapping that include theory, hands-on exercises, and semester projects.

Portfolio

GEOIDEA.RO The Geoportal

The GEOIDEA.RO project is the project I am working on for my PhD research. It is a collaboration work between the ETH Zurich, Switzerland and the Technical University of Civil Engineering in Bucharest, Romania. It aims at improving the scientific basis for open geodata model adoption in Romania. This project is co-financed by a grant from Switzerland through the Swiss Contribution to the enlarged European Union via the Swiss-Romanian Cooperation Programme.

  • GEodata Open Initiative for Development and Economic Advancement in Romania
  • Date: 2013 - 2015

GEOIDEA.RO The Geoportal Lite

With the GEOIDEA.RO project, we are providing all the data from the geoportal as WMS as well. This Geoportal Lite exemplify the use of our web services with third party libraries, here Leaflet. The code repository for this project is public.
